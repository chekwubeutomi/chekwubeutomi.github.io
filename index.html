About Me

I am a master's student at the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS) South Africa, specializing in AI for Science with a particular focus on quantum computing applications. My academic journey is supported by Google DeepMind, enabling me to explore cutting-edge research at the intersection of artificial intelligence and quantum systems.

My research interests lie in developing and implementing quantum algorithms, exploring quantum machine learning, and applying computational methods to solve complex scientific problems. I'm particularly passionate about understanding how quantum computing can accelerate discoveries in chemistry, materials science, and optimization.

Beyond quantum computing, I work on developing transformer-based NLP models for low-resource African languages, contributing to making AI more accessible and representative of diverse linguistic communities.
